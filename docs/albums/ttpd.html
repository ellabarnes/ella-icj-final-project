Listen to The Tortured Poets Department on Spotify.

The Tortured Poets Department was released on April 19, 2024 -- the day after my birthday -- under Republic Records, and produced by Taylor Swift, Jack Antonoff, Aaron Dessner, and Patrik Berger. The Anthology vesion of the album was released with 15 additional songs. My favorite song off the album is "I Hate It Here."
