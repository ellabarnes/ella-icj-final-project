The Tortured Poets Department

7.9 / 10

Listen to The Tortured Poets Department

The Tortured Poets Department was released on April 19, 2024 - the day after my birthday - under Republic Records, and was produced by Taylor Swift, Jack Antonoff, Aaron Dessner, and Patrik Berger. The Anthology version was released with 15 additional songs. My favorite song off the album is "I Hate It Here."
