1989

9.21 / 10

Listen to 1989

1989 was released October 27, 2014 under Big Machine Records, and was produced by Max Martin, Taylor Swift, Shellback, Jack Antonoff, Ryan Tedder, Noel Zancanella, Ali Payami, Nathan Chapman, Imogen Heap, and Mattman & Robin. The deluxe version of this album was released on January 1, 2014 with an additional 6 tracks, 3 of which are voice memos. 1989 was Swift's first pop album. My favorite song off this album is "You Are In Love."
