Listen to Red

Red was released October 22, 2012 under Big Machine Records, and was produced by Taylor Swift, Nathan Chapman, Jeff Bhasker, Dann Huff, Jacknife Lee, Max Martin, Shellback, Butch Walker, and Dan Wilson. The deluxe version of this album was released with an additional six tracks. My favorite song off this album is "Begin Again."
