Midnights

9.09 / 10

Listen to Midnights

Midnights was released October 21, 2022 under Republic Records, and was produced by Taylor Swift, Jack Antonoff, Sounwave, Jahaan Sweet, and Keanu Beats. There are four additional variations of Midnights (3am Edition, Lavender Edition, The Til Dawn Edition, and the limited CD only sold as the East Rutherford Eras Tour shows) with 11 additional songs in total. My favorite song off the album is "Glitch."
