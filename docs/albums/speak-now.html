Speak Now

9.46 / 10

Listen to Speak Now

Speak Now was released October 25, 2010 under Big Machine Records, and was produced by Nathan Chapman and Taylor Swift. This album was written entirely by Swift, since at the time many people did not believe she wrote her own music. The deluxe version was released with six additional tracks. My favorite song off this album is "Innocent."
