Lover

8.88 / 10

Listen to Lover

Lover was released on August 23, 2019 under Republic Records, and was produced by Taylor Swift, Jack Antonoff, Joel Little, Louis Bell, and Frank Dukes. Alongside the album, Swift released four different diary notebooks for fans to purchase that included excerpts from her diaries and unreleased song lyrics. Lover was her first album under Republic. My favorite song off this album is "It's Nice To Have A Friend."
